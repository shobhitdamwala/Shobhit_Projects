Projects

Property Rental System

The Property Rental System is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline the rental process. It allows users to browse and search for rental properties, view detailed property listings, and manage rental applications. The system simplifies property management and enhances the rental experience by providing an efficient platform for both landlords and tenants to interact and complete rental transactions.

Online Service System

The Online Home Services System is a comprehensive platform designed to offer a wide array of home services, much like Urban Company. It connects users with professional service providers for various needs, including cleaning, plumbing, electrical repairs, and more. Through the platform, users can easily browse available services, book appointments, and select providers based on ratings and reviews. The system features a streamlined booking process, real-time tracking of service progress, and secure payment options to ensure a hassle-free experience. Additionally, it offers features like personalized recommendations, service guarantees, and customer support, making it a go-to solution for managing home maintenance and improvement needs efficiently.

Online College Management System

The Online College Management System is a sophisticated digital platform designed to streamline the administration of educational institutions. This system offers a centralized solution for managing academic processes, including student enrollment, course scheduling, grade tracking, and faculty management. With user-friendly interfaces and real-time data access, it enhances operational efficiency and supports effective communication between students, faculty, and administration. Key features include automated attendance tracking, online grade reporting, and a robust scheduling tool, all designed to simplify college management and improve the overall educational experience.
